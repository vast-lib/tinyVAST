# Data to analyze sponge-coral-fish associations

Data used in [Thorson et al.
2025](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/geb.70035) and copied
from [Zenodo](https://zenodo.org/records/15001896) to allow inclusion as
a package vignette. This includes bottom-trawl samples of rockfish and
flatfish from the Alaska Fisheries Science Center in the Gulf of Alaska
and Aleutian Islands, and drop-camera samples of rockfish, flatfish,
sponges, and corals from the Alaska Fisheries Science Center and funded
by the Deep Sea Coral Initiative.

## Usage

``` r
data(alaska_sponge_coral_fish)
```
